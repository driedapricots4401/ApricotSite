The Global Face of Malatya

From Nature to Export:
Toraman's Quality

As Toraman's Dried Apricots, we have been bringing the unique flavors of Malatya to the global market since 2010, backed by deep experience in the dried food sector.

Today, we operate with a 100% customer satisfaction principle across an extensive export network reaching from Germany, France, the UK, and the USA to Israel and Japan.

We take pride in representing domestic production on a global scale by delivering almost our entire output to four corners of the world, without compromising on international hygiene and quality standards.
